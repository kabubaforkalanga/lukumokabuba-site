About

Lukumo Kabuba is a committed community member standing as an independent candidate for Councillor of Kalanga Ward in Kalulushi Constituency. He is passionate about improving the lives of residents through practical solutions, transparency, and active community engagement.

Key Priorities

  • Improving access to healthcare facilities
  • Empowering local markets and businesses
  • Developing infrastructure and roads
  • Creating opportunities for youth
  • Strengthening community engagement
